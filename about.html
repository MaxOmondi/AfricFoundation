About Us

Mission: At dAF, we aim to democratize education by providing affordable and easily accessible educational resources. We particularly target children, especially girls, from developing countries and low-income families. We believe that every child, regardless of their socioeconomic background, deserves access to quality education. Through our innovative approaches, we strive to break down barriers to learning, empower young minds, and foster equitable opportunities for all. By leveraging technology and community partnerships, we aim to create a world where every child can unlock their full potential and thrive in an inclusive and knowledge-driven society."

Vision: Our vision at dAF is to create a world where quality education is not a privilege, but a fundamental right accessible to every child, especially girls, from marginalized communities and low-income families. We envision a future where barriers to learning are dismantled, and educational resources are readily available and affordable to all, irrespective of their background. Through our relentless commitment to innovation, equity, and collaboration, we aspire to empower generations of learners, enabling them to realize their dreams, contribute meaningfully to society, and build a brighter, more inclusive future for themselves and their communities."
